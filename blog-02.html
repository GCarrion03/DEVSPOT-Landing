My journey to become an AWS Certified Solutions Architect Associate

Passed the aws certified developer Associate exam DVA-C01 (862/1000)

Yesterday I passed awscda exam, I've also completed the awscsaa and, oh boy this test is harder than the other one during the test I thought was going to fail, I had to guess on some questions and think about what is the most intuitive way to implement the things they were asking (I've been a developer for 8 years, 1 year interacting with beanstalk). So this test is no joke. Maybe I was a bit overconfident since I scored 932 on the AWSCSAA. Anyway here is a quick recap of my preparation.

Study materials: 

  • a cloud guru awscda course + free internet resources for practice tests(flashcards.io, blogs, examtopics).

Total study time 3 weeks (21 days) 1 hour each day:

  • Week 1 + 2: go thru the acg course and do the practice quizzes. 
  • Week 3: practice tests.

After I completed around 300 practice questions I assumed I was ready for the test, none of them were on it(i guess aws is doing a good job rotating the questions). The toughest questions for me where the ones related to CLI and cloudformation since I didn't have much time to play with the console or buid anything on aws.

Finally I'd like to give something back to the aws community by providing AWS-CDA aspirants a totally free exam simulator here.
